The Hemingway

Branding/Visual Identity

'The Hemingway' is a hypothetical bookstore and cafe in which I created a logo and visual identity for. For this project, I took into account my own connection to the atmosphere bookstores and cafes, which is comfort and calmness. Therefore, I aimed to create a visual identity which reflected the atmosphere of The Hemingway, through the muted colour palette and simple typography and patterns.
