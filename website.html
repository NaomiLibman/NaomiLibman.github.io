Wikipedia Article Re-design

Web/UX UI Design

The brief of this task was to re design a Wikipedia article of our choice based on how we perceived the contents of the article. I decided to use the Wikipedia article on Feminism, focusing in particular on the societal impacts sub-heading to educate users on the different perspectives of feminism from various individuals and communities. I also conducted my own research from external sites to extend on certain sub-headings and use this information to extend the interactivity of the website. Through using a combination of html, css and javascript I created a website for users to be able to learn about the impacts of feminism in different societies through interaction to show these various perspectives, accompanied by a bright colour pallete and fun, playful hand drawn style illustrations and gifs.
