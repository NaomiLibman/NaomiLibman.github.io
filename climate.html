Changing Climate

Branding/Visual Identity

'Visit Australia's Changing Climate' is a hypothetical brand idenity for the Changing Climate exhibition at the Australian Museum in Sydney. For this task, I decided to focus on how climate change particularly affects Australia by referencing popular Australian tourist destinations and reference tourism advertisements in a satirical way to show what the future could look like and the effects on these destinations if Climate Change became irreversible, hence encouraging audiences to view the exhibition and help prevent these loved destinations from irreversible damage caused by climate change. Through utilising simple typography, a muted colour pallete and digital illustration, I created this visual identity to highligh my concept.
