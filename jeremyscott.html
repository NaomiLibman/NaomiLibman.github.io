Renegade Series – Jeremy Scott (VOL 1)

Publication Design

Volume 1 of Fashion Industry Broadcast’s Renegade series focuses on the autobiography of fashion designer and Moschino creative director Jeremy Scott. Jeremy Scott has always had a unique style since the beginning of his career, with designs that are full of colour, cartoon and pop culture references, shocking fashion critics and going against traditional fashion design guidelines. The spreads of this publication are designed to reflect Scott’s outgoing, loud designs, with various colours and textures complementing the sourced imagery, all whilst adhering to Fashion Industry Broadcast’s original style guidelines.
