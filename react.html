#PlanAgainstFatigue

Branding/Campaign

#PlanAgainstFatigue is a road safety campaign with RE:ACT aimed at 18-25-year-old drivers. Its goal is to prevent fatigue-related accidents during long-distance drives on rural roads and highways. Rather than just advising against driving, the campaign encourages young drivers to plan ahead to reduce the risk of accidents. The posters feature catchy slogans and playful illustrations to offer practical tips for planning before long drives.
